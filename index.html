What's Next?

Evolution of Cars.

Listed here will be a brief summery of the history of cars and how safety became a concern then how it improved throught the years.

Purchase

Back in the day in the 1900s, the only people who purchased a car was the wealthy just for the image and comfortabillity. Doctors have purhcases small cars because they still believed in Horses. Now a days we can purhcase a car online and get it delivered directly to our house in no time!

Safety

Back in the 1980s, they came with a invention that deployed a cution that deployed when impacted called a Airbag. During the mid 2000s Blind spot detection, Auto Braking, and a 8 point air bag system. Now we have a car that can drive itself with strict conditions. There are many laws and safety requirements cars need to pass in order to be sold to the public.

Creativity

In the Early 2000s, a movie "The Fast and The Furious" has released and people started to put quite alot of accessories and many other "performance looking" items in our cars. To this day there are still people who do that but there is nothing wrong with that. Anyone can design or create their own car in the way they want it.

Car Fax

Buying a preowned car used to be tough. Without any correct paperwork, no one will know what was exactly done to the vehicle or if it had the right services done to maintain the car running optimal. Now there is a website Carfax that can give the history of the car, what the current title is and where it was been during the life of its time being.

Detailing

The first ever car detailing came in the 1800s. Every day the detailing products have been improving with new products, equipment, andn method to use on a daily basis. With new technology and methods of clean up, cars can be kept clean and tidey by keeping the water off and easy wash off products. Here is a company who does detailing.

What's Next?

EV or Electic Vehicles are the new wave. Most car manucaturers are creating more and more electic cars to be more eco and reduce the usage of Oils. For car enthusiasts, this can be looked as a bad idea although there are some electric sports cars and soon there will be electric supercars like the Tesla Roadster.
